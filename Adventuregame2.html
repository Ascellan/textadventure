Time-dimension machine

By Alex Hill

You’re sitting in a shack that you’ve managed to build yourself after everything fell apart. This is the only place you know where you feel some echo of a feeling that even remotely resembles safety.

It’s just… All gone.

Your heart feels empty, you know it's there but now it's just a remnant of once a vibrant organ in a dusty shell of what feels like an ancient machinery, waiting for a breath of wind to crush it finally into pieces.

At least you have one loyal friend beside you.
